Surveillance Through Android Apps

This project shows how an attacker could develop a malicious Android app to spy on a phone. We will build a fake App, run it safely on a fake device to demonstrate how remote access is obtained through permissions like (contacts, camera, mic, messages). 82% of Android devices are vulnerable to at least one of 25 known Android OS flaws. The write-up explains practical steps to spot malicious apps and strengthen devices so individual/business reputation and customer trust are protected.
