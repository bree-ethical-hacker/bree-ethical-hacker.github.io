Hey, It's Bree

I'm a Jr.

My vision is to create a community, The Black Widow Community, where cybersecurity beginners interested in offensive security stop feeling stuck between knowing cyber and doing cyber, where hands-on experience builds portfolios that don't just showcase skills, but will open doors for you, yes you who's reading this.

My mission is to bridge the gap between theory and the real world, while creating accountability that transforms inconsistent learning into inevitable growth.

Hey reader, let me give you a bit of info about myself. At 18, I moved across the globe alone to find out what it would really take to build a life from scratch. I had no connections, no technical background, and no clear path into cybersecurity. I struggled with the weight of competition, and no matter how many degrees and certificates I earned, I still doubted my abilities.

I know exactly how that feels, which is why I built The Black Widow Community to support beginners in offensive security. So you can become an undeniable Ethical Hacker.
