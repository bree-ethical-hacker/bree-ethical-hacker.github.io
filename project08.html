Accessing Usernames & Passwords

This project shows how 15% of cyber attacks occur, with a simple phishing email. It demonstrates how an attacker can send a fake email linked to a cloned website, tricking users into entering their login details. Once their username and password are stolen, these credentials can be used to access business/personal accounts. This breach causes direct wire fraud loss, even though it's a one-time hit to the balance sheet, a breach that exposes sensitive client data or trade secrets can trigger a domino effect of legal litigation, massive regulatory fines, and permanent brand devaluation.
