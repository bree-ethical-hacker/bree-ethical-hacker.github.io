Breaking An Email System

This lab shows, in a safe test environment, exactly how the email system attack is done and, more importantly, how to stop it. You will see the exploit happen in a virtual machine, understand what went wrong, and get clear, practical steps to fix the problem, detect future attacks faster, and recover if needed. The project helps reduce risk, improve incident response, and support compliance. References: Ransomware recovery = $1.82M (SC Media), Business Email Compromise (BEC) fraud = multi-billion dollar problem (FBI/IC3).
