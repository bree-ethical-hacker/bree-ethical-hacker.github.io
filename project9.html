Hunting Security Gaps in Businesses

This project shows the first step needed to perform most calculated attacks. It will demonstrate how attackers find weaknesses in a business by scanning what’s publicly visible. By identifying what’s publicly visible and fixing it early, a company can massively reduce its risk of being breached.

The full write-up will walk you through how attackers gather critical business information, what impact it can have on your business, and practical ways to prevent it, all backed by credible references.
